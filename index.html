Certificate Of Compliance

A Electrical Certificate of Compliance (COC) is a document that verifies that the electrical installations such as the plugs, lights, DB-board, geyser, and wiring in a home comply with the legislated requirements as detailed in the Occupational Health and Safety Act.

Inverter Installation

With load shedding still being part of our reality in South Africa for some time to come, we have the best power solutions for your needs. Whether you are a business or a household, COC-Inverters have a range of battery backup, solar ready solutions, that will suit your unique needs and keep you protected from unexpected outages and load shedding.

Electrical Rewiring

At Boulo Electrical, we understand that your electrical system is the lifeline of your property. Over time, wear and tear, outdated wiring, or faulty electrical components can compromise safety and efficiency. That's where our Electrical Rewiring Services come into play.
