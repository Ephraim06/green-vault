About Us

Established in 1989, GREENVAULT GLOBAL ENTERPRISE is a company that seamlessly combines both theoretical expertise and hands-on experience in the fields of electrical and construction works. Our team comprises individuals with diverse qualifications, including BSc and National N Diplomas in civil and electrical engineering (heavy current), as well as certifications as qualified electricians, installation electricians, and master installation electricians. We are also proud holders of certificates in medium voltage (MV) and low voltage (LV) electrical work, and our team excels in administrative and project management roles.

Generations of Exceptional Service

In 2019, we expanded our horizons by venturing into the logistics industry. Our logistics division is responsible for efficiently transporting coal from Masemanzi mining to power stations, catering to the energy needs of the Mpumalanga region. Additionally, we proudly provided a 40m3 front loader to enhance our logistical operations.

In June 2020, we embarked on a new journey by becoming part of the supply chain for chrome transportation. Our dedicated efforts led to the successful delivery of chrome from the Northwest region to Richards Bay, in partnership with KWS Logistics.

Milestone Investment in Property Development

Taking a significant step forward in 2020, Boulo Electrical CC made a strategic investment by acquiring land in Brooklyn, Cape Town. This investment marked the beginning of our inaugural property development project, with a substantial value of R19,760,000.00. This project signifies our commitment to growth and progress, not only in the realm of electrical and construction services but also in property development, as we strive to contribute to the development and transformation of the communities we serve.

At Boulo Electrical CC, our journey is characterized by continuous growth, diversification, and a relentless pursuit of excellence in all that we do. We look forward to many more years of success and service to our valued clients and partners.

Compliance

We comply with all Occupational Health and Safety Act and Labour Relations Act regulations. We are registered with the Electrical Contracting Board of South Africa (ECB) and are listed in the Central Supply Database, as well as WCSD, NHBRC, CIDB, COIDA, and BIBC.
